Mission goal
============

The mice are not happy: a cat generator has been installed in the 
cellar!

Find and kill the cat generator, and remove all the hidden cats that 
populate the cellar.

NOTE: the generator is a process, while the cats themselves are 
files.


Useful commands
===============

ps
  list the process that are currently executed by the shell

kill N
  kill the process with number ("PID") N
